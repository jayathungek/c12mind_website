Who are we?

c12mind provides an AI-powered platform that simulates and optimizes the upcycling of carbon-rich waste (textiles, biomass, plastics) into high performance materials like activated carbon, CNTs, and graphene.

Pain points tackled

Difficulty sourcing high-performance, low-carbon carbon materials due to regulatory pressure, supply chain risk, and R&D inefficiencies.

Why us?

Unlike traditional carbon suppliers, we deliver AI-designed, application-specific materials from circular inputs, faster, greener, and performance-tuned.

Validated in the lab

Our lab-scale validation shows successful conversion of waste into activated carbon with commercial-grade performance metrics; pilot engagements are in progress.
